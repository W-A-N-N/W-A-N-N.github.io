Legend:

Welcome to WAAC, the Worlds Adrift Alliance Codex. Powered by WANN, the Worlds Adrift News Network. Find on the side the toggle-able buttons to take you to that alliances information.

  • Buttons coloured like This are Passive or Allied
  • Buttons coloured like This are Neutral
  • Buttons coloured like This are Aggresive
  • Buttons coloured like This are Hostile or Enemies

In many cases official data about the alliances was unavailable, this will be marked with a . In these cases we've inferred the data from other sources!


Interpretating Neutrality:

In WAAC we've colour coded everything to make it simple but in many cases our source listed as "Neutral" or some kind of status which we turned into a number, therefore understanding neutralitiy is important. For example a 10 ranked will veiw neutrals as targets while a 1 would view neutrals as allies, though this is a simplified example, it rings true! The aggression number on the allaince refers to how they treat neutrals. As we've asumed that ungiven alliances are neutral.
